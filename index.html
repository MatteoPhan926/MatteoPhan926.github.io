A GPT-2 Inference Engine in Pure C & CUDA
Matteo Phan
C / CUDA / Roofline Shipped · Jan – Jul 2026
decode @ ctx=128, fp16, true M=1 511.7 tok/s
80% of llama.cpp F16 (636.4 tok/s) · 4.8× PyTorch eager

Single-model, forward-inference-only GPT-2-124M engine built from scratch. Every kernel is gated against a HuggingFace fp16 oracle before it is timed, and checked against a microbenchmarked ceiling. Weight-only INT8 halves weight bytes but buys 1.00× (compute-bound on discarded rows). Fused LayerNorm+matmul was 2.9% slower and reverted.

NeuInteract — Amortized Contact Deformation on 3D Gaussians
Matteo Phan, ML4U Lab
3DGS / FEniCSx / FEM Targeting CVPR 2027

A forward, amortized, controllable contact-deformation operator acting natively on Gaussian density.
