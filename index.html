A GPT-2 Inference Engine in Pure C & CUDA

C CUDA Roofline Shipped · Jan – Jul 2026
decode @ ctx=128, fp16, true M=1 511.7 tok/s
80% of llama.cpp F16 (636.4 tok/s), matched precision · 4.8× PyTorch eager
  • Single-model, forward-inference-only GPT-2-124M engine built from scratch: pure-C fp32 reference → CUDA port → tiled GEMM → flash attention → INT8 → KV cache with a true M=1 GEMV.
  • Every kernel is gated against a HuggingFace fp16 oracle before it is timed, and every number is checked against a ceiling microbenchmarked on the GPU itself — including one ceiling I had assumed, measured, and found wrong by 1.55×.
  • The negative results are in the repo. Weight-only INT8 halves the weight bytes and buys 1.00×: at batch=1 the kernel is compute-bound on the rows it throws away. Fused LayerNorm+matmul is 2.9% slower and was reverted.

Label Efficiency & Synthetic Data for Vietnamese Scene-Text OCR

PyTorch Synthetic data Pre-registered Shipped · Sep – Dec 2025
recognition CER @ 10% label budget · VinText 16.51% → 13.73%
measured against augmentation-matched control arms, not a bare baseline
  • Pre-registered, ablation-controlled study: a custom Vietnamese text generator plus a three-axis NFD scorer to quantify how synthetic each sample really is.
  • The result refuted the hypothesis it was designed to test. The degradation stack is not the lever. The gain comes from sequence-level length signal that repairs premature decoder termination on long strings — shown by control arms, not asserted.

NeuInteract — Amortized Contact Deformation on 3D Gaussians

3DGS FEniCSx FEM In progress · targeting CVPR 2027
  • A forward, amortized, controllable contact-deformation operator that acts natively on Gaussian density — physics-grounded and trajectory-respecting.
  • Ran a pre-registered FEM validation campaign (FEniCSx, mixed (u, p) Taylor–Hood, cross-solver verified) that characterized contact-induced surface bulge as a linear incompressible-confinement effect (Dbulge ≈ 3a, bonded-base gated) — which eliminated the project's own original novelty claim and forced a re-scope.
  • Adversarial prior-art occupancy analysis to locate what is actually left to contribute.
